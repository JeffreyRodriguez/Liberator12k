== Common Dimensions

.General Dimension Guidelines
[cols="1,1,1"]
|===
|Purpose|Inch|Metric

|Small Pins
|3/32"
|2.5mm

|Medium Pins
|1/4"
|6mm

|Small Screws
|#8-32
|M4

|Medium Screws
|1/4"-20
|M6

|Large Screws
|1/2"-13
|12mm

|Square Rods
|1/4"
|6mm
|===

Official kits are inch-standard except small pins are 2.5mm.
3/32" is just under 2.5mm, and a #39 drill bit is just over 2.5mm.
